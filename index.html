Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "Английский за 16" (English in 16) (Bundle ID: com.tenx.english16). By using this App, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Does not collect device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or similar)
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • Does not collect IP addresses
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Does not require user registration or account creation

2. Local Data Storage

All data generated while using the App is stored exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • Learning progress (completed lessons, exercise scores)
  • Vocabulary review status and flashcard data
  • App preferences (theme selection, language settings)
  • Purchase status (unlocked content)

This data never leaves your device. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses only Apple Store Kit (In-App Purchases). When you make a purchase:

  • The transaction is processed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID
  • We do not receive or store your payment information
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to the transaction

The App does not use: advertising networks, analytics platforms, social media integrations, crash reporting, or push notifications.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected, it complies with COPPA and GDPR for minors. No parental consent is required.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted externally, the risk of data breaches is minimal.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date above.

7. Contact Us

We will respond within 30 days.
